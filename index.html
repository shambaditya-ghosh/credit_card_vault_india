Expert Tips

Smart Credit Card Tips

Maximize benefits, avoid pitfalls & build a powerful credit card strategy

TIP 01

Always Pay Full Balance

Credit card interest rates in India range from 36–42% per annum. Carrying a balance immediately wipes out all your rewards. Always pay the full outstanding amount before the due date.

TIP 02

Stack Cards for Maximum Returns

Use Amazon Pay ICICI for Amazon, Axis Ace for utility bills, HDFC Millennia for Swiggy/Zomato, and a travel card for flights. Each card maximizes returns in its category.

TIP 03

Use SmartBuy for 10X Points

HDFC's SmartBuy portal gives 10X reward points on travel, shopping & dining bookings. This can make even mid-tier cards like Regalia offer premium-level returns.

TIP 04

Transfer Points Before Expiry

Most reward points expire in 2–3 years. Regularly transfer to airline miles or redeem against vouchers. HDFC RP never expire, but Amex MR points must be actively managed.

TIP 05

Monitor Credit Utilization

Keep your credit utilization below 30% of total limit. High utilization hurts your CIBIL score significantly. Spread spends across multiple cards to maintain a healthy ratio.

TIP 06

Meet Annual Fee Waiver Targets

Most cards waive the annual fee if you meet the spend milestone. Plan your annual purchases around this — make sure rent, insurance, and large buys go through the card.

TIP 07

Avoid Cash Advances

Cash advance on credit cards attracts 2.5–3% fee immediately plus 42%+ annual interest from day one. No grace period. This is one of the most expensive financial mistakes.

TIP 08

Set Up Auto-Pay for Minimum

As a safety net, set auto-pay for at least the minimum amount due. This prevents late payment fees and protects your CIBIL score even if you forget to pay manually.
